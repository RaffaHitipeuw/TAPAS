Who are we?

TAPAS (Tanpa Asap) is an app-based e-bike rental service that allows you to easily rent an electric bike through our app as part of our effort to reduce pollution and traffic congestion in major cities across Indonesia.
Our Mission
Aims to provide easy and reliable access to electric bikes through smart, app-based technology. In order to reduce pollution and traffic congestion in big cities with electric transportations. We strive to support sustainable city development, encourage healthier lifestyles, and raise environmental awareness among communities. By expanding our network of e-bike stations across major cities in Indonesia, TAPAS is committed to making green transportation more convenient, impactful, and accessible to everyone.
